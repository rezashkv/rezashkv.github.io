I am a 3rd year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Maryland, College Park. Currently, I am advised by Dr. Heng Huang. My research interests are in the areas of Deep Learning, and Generative Models. I am particularly interested in developing efficient deep learning models that can be deployed on resource constrained systems and edge devices. Prior to joining UMD, I received my B.Sc. in Computer Engineering from Sharif University of Technology.
