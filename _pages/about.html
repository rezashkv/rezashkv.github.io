I am a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Maryland, College Park. Currently, I am under advised by Dr. Heng Huang. My research interests are in the areas of machine learning and computer vision. I am particularly interested in developing efficient and robust deep learning models for visual recognition tasks. Prior to joining UMD, I received my B.Sc. in Computer Engineering from Sharif University of Technology. I also worked in the industry as a Data Scientist for two years.
